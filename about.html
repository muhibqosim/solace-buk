who we are

Committed to improving the standard and competence of our members while providing doors for mentorships and opportunities. Opened to sponsorship and partnership.

Mission Statement

Our mission at Solace Chambers is to provide a dynamic and supportive environment where law students can develop their skills, knowledge, and character, empowering them for successful careers in the legal industry and leadership roles.

Our Vision

Our vision is to continually be a leading student-based Chambers, known for our commitment to building competence, excellence, integrity, and professionalism. We envision a future where members, as aspirants to the bar, leverage mentorship for a fulfilling legal career.

Our Objectives

1. Foster Excellence: Provide a supportive environment that nurtures legal excellence, innovation, and advocacy among law students.

2. Develop Skills: Equip members with comprehensive and up-to-date knowledge, legal skills, and best practices required for success in the legal profession.

3. Promote Professional Development: Offer training, mentorship, and networking opportunities to enhance members' career prospects and professional growth.

OUR DIRECTORATES AND Committees

Behind the functionality of Solace Chambers are our standing Directorates comprising of commiitted, tireless members.

Research and Litigation Directorate

At the heart of its operation is the Directorate of Research and Litigation, that drive the Chambers' intellectual and advocacy pursuits. The Directorate ensures continuous learning and a positive impact of our members with a quest of raising the bar.

The Directorate organises and facilitates a series of insightful programs and events, spanning across different aspects of law with the aim of equipping members with the knowledge essential for sound legal reasoning. The Directorates also focuses on advocacy training, procedural law, and trial techniques, preparing respected partners for real-world legal practice.

The Directorate forms a backbone of the Chambers, fostering a culture of excellence, student legal training, and professional readiness. At the realm of the Directorate lies the Director of Research and Dispute Resolution, the two Assistants and a number of committed respected members as members of the Directorate.

The Directorate specifically engage in activities including:

  • • Moot and Mock Competition
  • • Weekly Legal Bit Publication
  • • Procedural Quiz Competition
  • • Weekly Advocacy Clases
